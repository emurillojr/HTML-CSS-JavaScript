Week Seven Homework

Assignment Details

In this homework assignment you will use javascript to collect information from the DOM. Then you will either change or display that information in the browser's console. This assignment will give you practice using the document.getElementById(); method of the document object. It will also provide practice in the creation of variables and functions.

The first steps will involve gathering the data and storing it in variables. The information you will be collecting and storing in variables is:

  1. The innerHTML of the p with the id="greeting"
  2. The style.color of the the figcaption
  3. The value of the text field
  4. The src of the img with the id="machoman"

In the final step you will be writing a function called logIt() that will be called upon the clicking of the submit button. This function will display the information in the console.
